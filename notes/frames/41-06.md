Tell the Disciples 41-06
==========================


Then the **angel** told the women, “Go and tell the **disciples**,
'**Jesus** has **risen** from the **dead** and **he will go to Galilee
ahead of you**.'”

Translation Notes:
------------------

-   **he will go to Galilee ahead of you**- That is, “he will meet you
    in Galilee” or, “he will be in Galilee when you get there.”
    “You” here is plural, including the apostles and other disciples.
