The Small Debt 29-05
======================


“But when the **servant** went out from the king, he found a **fellow
servant** who owed him a debt worth four months’ wages. The servant
grabbed his fellow servant and said, ‘Pay me the money that you owe
me!’”

Translation Notes:
------------------

- -   **fellow servant** - This refers to another man who was also a
    servant of the king.
